3. PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that can reasonably be used to identify you or that is associated with you (“Personal Information”). The Personal Information we collect may include:

  • Identifiers you provide directly: name, email address, phone number, and birthday.
  • Account information: usernames, passwords, account settings, and preferences.
  • Transaction & reward activity: brand/store relationships, reward accrual and redemption activity, and related in-app actions.
  • Device and usage information (collected automatically): IP address, device type, operating system, app version, browser type, diagnostics, and log data used to maintain and improve the Services.
  • Communications: your correspondence with us (e.g., support emails).

We do not currently sell Personal Information. If our practices change, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, provide you with appropriate notices and choices.

4. HOW WE USE PERSONAL INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and operate the Services: including account creation, authentication, and reward accrual/redemption.
  • Identity within portals: we use your name to identify users to brand/store users in our portals as part of normal operations.
